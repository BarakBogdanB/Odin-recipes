Buckwheat

Healthy food

One of the healthiest of foods while still being tasty

Very easy to make

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of Buckwheat
  • 4 cups of water

Steps:

  1. Clean your 1 cup of Buckwheat
  2. Boil your 4 cups of water
  3. Salt the water
  4. Put the clean Buckwheat into the boiling water
  5. Lower the gas to lowest heat and place a lid over
  6. Wait for 30 minutes
  7. After the initial 30 minutes come and check the water level every 5 minutes
  8. Once the water is gone you can turn off the gas and put some butter on top of the fresh Buckwheat
  9. Wait for the butter to melt
  10. Server the buckwheet however you like
