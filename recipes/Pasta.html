Pasta

Carbs food

Basically is bread

Hard to make

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pasta maker

Steps:

  1. Make a nest with the flour on a clean work surface.
  2. Use your hands to gently mix in the flour.
  3. Knead the dough
  4. When the dough comes together, shape it into a ball and wrap it in plastic wrap.
  5. Let the dough rest at room temperature for 30 minutes.
  6. After the dough rests, slice it into 4 pieces.
  7. Use a rolling pin or your hands to gently flatten one into an oval disk.
  8. Run it through the widest setting of your pasta maker
  9. Fold it in half lengthwise to form a rectangle.
  10. Roll it out to your desired thickness
  11. Repeat these steps with the remaining dough pieces.
  12. Cut and cook the pasta.
